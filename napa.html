Simple Japanese Napa Cabbage Stir Fry

Description

A simple Japanese style napa cabbage stir fry suitable for all sorts of customisation

Ingredients for Serving Size

  • 100g Napa cabbage
  • 1 Shiitake mushroom
  • 1 slice Smoked bacon
  • 0.5 tsp Extra-virgin olive oil
  • 0.06 tsp Salt
  • 0.03 tsp Ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp Japanese soy sauce

Preparation

  • Cut napa cabbage crosswise into 5 cm long pieces, cut tough thick pieces in half or thirds lengthwise
  • Cut off stem from shiitake and then thinly slice the caps
  • Cut the bacon into 1.3 cm wide strips

Cooking

  1. Heat pan on medium heat, once hot add bacon and stir fry
  2. Once bacon is cooked, add thick pieces of napa cabbage
    3. and sprinkle in half of prepared salt
  3. Stir to mix and then cover and cook on medium-low heat for 2 minutes
  4. Open lid and stir, add shiitake and tender, leafy pieces of naba cabbage. Sprinkle in the rest of the salt and stir
  5. Cover and cook for 3-4 minutes, check if tough cabbage pieces are cooked through
  6. If not stir and then cover for additional 1-2 minutes
  7. Season with 0.03 tsp freshly ground black pepper and 0.5 tsp soy sauce. Stir
  8. Transfer to a plate and serve

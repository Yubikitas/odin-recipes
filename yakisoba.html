Simple Yakisoba

Description

A simple yakisoba recipe suitable for those without much cooking experience. The recipe is suitable for simple modifications to suit your needs and wants, the sauce's sour tang goes well with all sorts of ingredients.

Ingredients for serving size

1

  • 27 ml
    Yakisoba sauce
  • 100 g
    Sliced pork belly or preferred protein
  • 47 g
    Onion
  • 33 g
    Carrot
  • 75 g
    Green cabbage
  • 0.67
    Scallions
  • 1
    Shiitake mushrooms
  • 0.67
    Tbsp Neutral oil
  • black pepper
  • 1 serving
    Pre-steamed yakisoba noodles

Preparation

  • Cut the pork belly to 2.5 cm pieces
  • Cut the onion to 6 mm slices
  • Julienne the carrot into 5 cm strips
  • Remove the core of the cabbage and cut the cabbage wedge into 2.5 cm wide pieces
  • Chop green onions into 5 cm pieces
  • Remove the shiitakestems and cut into 6 mm slices

Cooking

  1. Heat the pan on medium heat and add 0.67 tbsp neutral oil when hot
  2. Add the serving of yakisoba noodles
  3. Loosen the noodles gently without breaking them into short pieces
  4. When the noodles are completely loosened, transfer them to a plate
  5. Add the sliced pork belly to the hot pan and season with black pepper
  6. Stir fry until the pork belly is no longer pink
  7. Add onion slices and cook for 1-2 minutes whilst separating the onion layers
  8. Add carrots and cook for 1-2 minutes
  9. Add cabbage and mushroom slices, cook until tender
  10. Add green onions and cook for 1 minute
  11. Put cooked yakisoba noodles on top of the protein and vegetable. Then drizzle 26.67 ml yakisoba sauce on the noodles
  12. Toss the noodles with a pair of tongs to combine the noodles and ingredients. When the sauce is well distributed transfer the finished noodles to a plate and serve
